About

I'm Bismith, a cyber security analyst and digital forensics investigator currently pursuing an integrated BSc. MSc in Cyber Forensics at NFSU Gandhinagar (2022–2027). My work sits at the intersection of forensic analysis, network security and IoT penetration testing — I enjoy pulling apart systems to understand exactly how and why something happened, then explaining it clearly to people who need to act on it.

I've worked hands-on with case studies involving metadata analysis, audio and image authentication, and forensic image acquisition from mobile devices and laptops during my internship at CFSL Pune. I'm currently exploring IoT vulnerability scanning and security testing as my focus area moving forward.
