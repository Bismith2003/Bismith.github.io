About

I'm Bismith, a cyber security analyst and digital forensics investigator currently pursuing an integrated BSc. MSc in Cyber Forensics at NFSU Gandhinagar (2022–2027). I'm currently exploring IoT vulnerability scanning and security testing as my focus area moving forward.
